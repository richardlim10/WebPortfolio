About Me

I'm a recent graduate of Villanova University with a B.S. in Statistics with a minor in Math, and I'm currently working as a Data Analyst at a360Media. I have a programming background and am skilled in languages like Python, R, Java, HTML, and I frequently use other tools like Looker, Tableau, and Microsoft Office.

I am passionate about programming and data, and love everything related to sports. My love for Data Analytics and sports has led me to create a Basketball Analytics Twitter page on which I post projects and dashboards using NBA data. You can find my account by clicking the Twitter icon in the header of this page.

In my free time, I enjoy playing sports (Soccer and Basketball), reading (my favorite genres are non-fiction and autobiographies), playing instruments (piano and guitar), and analyzing movies (my favorite movie is Inglorious Basterds).
